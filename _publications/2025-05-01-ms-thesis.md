My M.S. thesis examines plasma exchange between Earth and space — how charged particles escape from the ionosphere — covering theoretical foundations, modeling approaches from classical to contemporary frameworks, and mechanisms such as ambipolar electric fields and wave–particle interactions. Advised by Prof. Raluca Ilie.
