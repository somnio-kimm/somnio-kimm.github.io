Habilis-β is a fast-motion, long-lasting on-device vision–language–action (VLA) model designed for real-world deployment. In 1-hour continuous-run evaluations it achieves strong performance under the PRP metrics, compared with π₀.₅ in both simulation and the real world. (Tommoro Robotics; cs.RO / cs.LG.)
