The three **elementary row operations** are:

1. **Swap** two rows: $R_i \leftrightarrow R_j$.
2. **Scale** a row by a non-zero constant: $R_i \to c R_i$, $c \neq 0$.
3. **Add** a multiple of one row to another: $R_i \to R_i + c R_j$.

**Theorem.** Elementary row operations do not change the solution set of a system.
