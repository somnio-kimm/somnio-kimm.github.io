I'm **Soo Min Kimm**, a researcher in **embodied AI and robot learning** at **Tommoro Robotics**. I hold an **M.S. in Electrical & Computer Engineering** and a **B.S. in Electrical Engineering** and **Mathematics** from the **University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign**.

I like turning hard ideas into small, working things — from vision–language–action models for robots down to circuits on a breadboard. *Deep Chat of Perplexity* is where I think out loud about them.
